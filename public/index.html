Welcome to the Open Forecast, the website dedicated to forecasting using open source software and more.

This website is created in order to provide support for some R packages, related to forecasting, developed by Ivan Svetunkov, lecturer of Marketing Analytics at Lancaster University, UK and a memeber of Centre for Marketing Analytics and Forecasting.

The website currently has links to posts on smooth and greybox packages and to the online textbook on ADAM (Advanced Dynamic Adaptive Model). The latter is dedicated to a new model, encompassing ARIMA, ETS and regression.
